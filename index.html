My specialization has not only enriched my knowledge but also enhanced my ability to contribute to discussions on legal and moral matters in various contexts.

I completed a Master's degree in Arabic & Islamiat from the University of Karachi in 2017, a specialized program designed to provide an in-depth understanding of Islamic studies and the Arabic language. This degree offered a comprehensive exploration of Islamic history, theology, and law, alongside advanced linguistic training in Arabic. Through rigorous coursework and research, I gained a deeper appreciation for the cultural and intellectual heritage of the Islamic world. This specialization not only enhanced my scholarly abilities but also prepared me to contribute meaningfully to academic and religious discourse.
